Toyota Supra

Speed • Performance • Style

Overview

The Toyota Supra is a legendary sports car that blends aggressive styling, thrilling performance, and cutting-edge technology. It's designed for car enthusiasts who crave speed and precision.

Key Features

  • Engine: 2.0L Turbo / 3.0L Turbo Inline-6
  • Transmission: 8-speed Automatic with Paddle Shifters
  • Drive: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)
  • Horsepower: 255–382 hp
  • Fuel Type: Petrol
  • Seating Capacity: 2
  • Interior: Digital Cockpit, Leather Sports Seats, 8.8" Touchscreen
  • Safety: Pre-Collision System, Lane Departure Warning, Adaptive Cruise Control

Variants Available

  • Supra 2.0 (Base)
  • Supra 3.0
  • Supra 3.0 Premium
  • A91 Edition (Special)

Price Range (2025 Estimate)

Starts around USD $45,000 and goes up to $60,000+ depending on variant and customization.

Why People Love Supra

  • Legendary design with modern upgrades
  • Powerful engine and sporty handling
  • Great performance-to-price ratio
  • Popular in motorsports and car culture
  • Highly customizable and tuner-friendly
