Toyota Hilux Revo

Power • Style • Reliability

Overview

The Toyota Hilux Revo is a rugged and powerful pickup truck produced by Toyota, known for its durability, off-road capabilities, and stylish design.

Key Features

  • Engine: 2.4L / 2.8L Diesel
  • Transmission: Manual & Automatic
  • Drive: 4x2 and 4x4 options
  • Horsepower: 148–201 hp
  • Fuel Type: Diesel
  • Seating Capacity: 5
  • Modern Interior with Touchscreen & Climate Control
  • Safety: ABS, Airbags, Traction Control

Variants in Pakistan

  • Revo G
  • Revo V
  • Revo Rocco (Top Variant)

Price Range (2025 Estimate)

Starts around PKR 1.2 Crore and increases with features and variant level.

Why People Love Revo

  • Strong build quality
  • Excellent off-road performance
  • High resale value
  • Modern rugged design
  • Reliable for city and rural use
