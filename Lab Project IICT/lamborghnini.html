Lamborghini Aventador

Power • Luxury • Performance

Overview

The Lamborghini Aventador is an iconic supercar known for its exotic design, raw power, and cutting-edge engineering. It is a symbol of status, speed, and performance, delivering an unforgettable driving experience.

Key Features

  • Engine: 6.5L V12 Naturally Aspirated
  • Transmission: 7-speed ISR Automated Manual
  • Drive: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)
  • Horsepower: 730 hp
  • Fuel Type: Petrol
  • Seating Capacity: 2
  • Interior: Premium Leather, Digital Instrument Cluster, Infotainment System
  • Safety: Carbon-Ceramic Brakes, Traction Control, Advanced Airbags

Variants Available

  • Aventador S
  • Aventador SVJ
  • Aventador SVJ Roadster
  • Aventador Ultimae (Final Edition)

Price Range (2025 Estimate)

Starts around USD $500,000 and can exceed $600,000+ depending on variant and customization.

Why People Love Aventador

  • Iconic Lamborghini design
  • Massive V12 performance
  • High-speed capability and luxury
  • Exclusive and attention-grabbing
  • Advanced aerodynamics and technology
