Audi S6

Luxury • Performance • Innovation

Overview

The Audi S6 is a luxury sport sedan that blends high performance with elegant design and cutting-edge technology. It offers a thrilling drive while maintaining comfort and sophistication.

Key Features

  • Engine: 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6 with Mild-Hybrid System
  • Transmission: 8-speed Tiptronic Automatic
  • Drive: Quattro All-Wheel Drive
  • Horsepower: 444 hp
  • Fuel Type: Petrol
  • Seating Capacity: 5
  • Interior: Virtual Cockpit, Leather Sports Seats, Ambient Lighting
  • Safety: Adaptive Cruise Control, Lane Assist, Parking Sensors

Variants Available

  • Audi S6 Standard
  • Audi S6 Prestige
  • Audi S6 Black Optic Package

Price Range (2025 Estimate)

Starts around USD $75,000 and increases with trim levels and options.

Why People Love Audi S6

  • Refined power and acceleration
  • Elegant, sporty design
  • Luxurious and tech-loaded interior
  • Strong safety and driver assistance features
  • Perfect mix of comfort and performance
